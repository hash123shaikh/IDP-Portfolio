# IDP Annual Update Checklist

Use this checklist when updating your Individual Development Plan each year.

## üìã Pre-Update Preparation

### Review Previous Year
- [ ] Read last year's IDP completely
- [ ] Review CHANGELOG.md for accomplishments
- [ ] Gather evidence of completed goals (papers, presentations, certificates)
- [ ] Note goals that weren't achieved and understand why
- [ ] Celebrate successes!

### Gather Materials
- [ ] CV/Resume (updated)
- [ ] List of publications/presentations

#### Annual Goals (Short-term)
- [ ] List 3-5 specific, measurable goals
- [ ] Ensure goals are achievable within 12 months
- [ ] Align with medium and long-term objectives
- [ ] Include both technical and soft skills

**Scoring Guide:**
- 10/10: All sections thoroughly updated with specific, measurable goals
- 7-9/10: Most sections updated, some goals need more specificity
- 4-6/10: Basic updates made, lacks detail or measurability
- 0-3/10: Minimal changes or very vague goals

## üí° Tips for Success

1. **Be Honest**: Don't inflate achievements or hide challenges
2. **Be Specific**: Use numbers, dates, and concrete examples
3. **Be Realistic**: Set challenging but achievable goals
4. **Be Reflective**: Learn from both successes and setbacks
5. **Be Forward-Looking**: Balance documenting past with planning future
6. **Be Organized**: Keep supporting documents (certificates, papers) accessible
7. **Be Proactive**: Seek opportunities aligned with your goals

---

**Remember**: Your IDP is a tool for YOUR benefit. The more honest and thorough you are, the more valuable it becomes for your professional development.
